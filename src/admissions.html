Admissions

Admission Criteria

  • Minimum GPA of 3.0 in high school or undergraduate studies
  • SAT score of 1200 or higher, or ACT score of 26 or higher
  • Strong performance in mathematics and science courses
  • English language proficiency for international students
  • Demonstrated interest in computer science field

Application Steps

  1. Complete the online application form
  2. Submit official transcripts from all educational institutions
  3. Provide standardized test scores (SAT/ACT)
  4. Write a personal statement explaining your interest in computer science
  5. Request letters of recommendation from teachers or mentors
  6. Submit portfolio or coding projects (optional but recommended)
  7. Attend an admissions interview if requested

Degree Programs

Bachelor of Science in Computer Science

A comprehensive four-year program covering software development, algorithms, databases, and web technologies.

Master of Science in Computer Science

Advanced program for graduate students specializing in AI, cloud computing, cybersecurity, or software engineering.

Certificate in Full-Stack Development

An intensive program designed for career changers seeking expertise in modern web application development.

PhD in Computer Science

Research-focused doctoral program for students interested in advancing the field through original research and innovation.
