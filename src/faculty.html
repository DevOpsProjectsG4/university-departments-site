About Our Faculty

The Computer Science Department is proud to have a diverse team of professors, lecturers, and researchers. Our faculty members are dedicated to advancing knowledge in computing while mentoring the next generation of innovators. With expertise ranging from Artificial Intelligence to Cybersecurity, they combine academic rigor with practical experience.
